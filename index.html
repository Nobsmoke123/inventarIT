Repair Return Policy

We at Inventar IT would appreciate a quick heads-up about the status of a job that has been fixed but our clients are still having issues with the repair. We would only attend to client complaints made within 3 days after the job was picked up from any of our stores. Please note, you'd be charged for a new repair service if you don't adhere to this policy!

Cash Return Policy

We at Inventar IT would have a return cash policy for products purchased from us if the product is still in good condition within 3 days of the purchase. Please note, you'd be charged for a new product if you don't adhere to this policy!

Gadgets Pick-Up Policy

We at Inventar IT would love our clients to pick up their completed or fixed jobs at the date agreed upon; any violation to this would incur or bring about a fee payment of N500 for each day the job stays at any of our offices.
